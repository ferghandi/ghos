Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy explains what the CricOne Android app collects, why, and who it is shared with. CricOne is published by Mysticcraftz ("we", "us").

The short version. CricOne has no accounts and no login. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number or location. We do not sell personal data. The app uses a random identifier to count live viewers, and shows ads that may use your device's advertising ID.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

None. CricOne has no sign-up, no login and no profile. We never ask for your name, email address, phone number, photos, contacts or precise location.

Information collected automatically

Stored only on your phone

Your theme choice, followed matches, and a record of which polls and quizzes you have already answered are kept in local storage on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes them.

2. Permissions the app requests

  • Internet and network state — to load scores and streams, and to tell you when you're offline.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — optional. Only used for match alerts. You can decline, and the app works normally.

CricOne does not request location, camera, microphone, contacts or storage permissions.

3. Advertising

CricOne is free and supported by advertising through CAS.AI, which mediates several ad networks (including Google AdMob, AppLovin, Unity Ads and others). These partners may access your device's advertising identifier and non-precise information about your device to select and measure ads.

We show a consent dialog where required (for example under GDPR). You can reset or delete your advertising ID at any time in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Read CAS.AI's policy: cas.ai/privacy-policy. Google's policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — Cloud Messaging, Firestore, Realtime Database, App Check. (policy)
  • Google Play services — including Play Integrity. (policy)
  • CAS.AI and its mediated ad networks — advertising.
  • Sportmonks — cricket scores and statistics. Requests reach Sportmonks through our own server, which does not forward any information about you.
  • Cloudflare — serves our API proxy. Like any web server, it processes IP addresses to route and cache requests.

5. Live video content

Some matches include links to video streams hosted by third parties. Those streams are not operated by us. When you open one, the hosting provider may receive your IP address and standard request information, governed by that provider's own policy — not this one.

6. Children

CricOne is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Viewer presence is deleted the moment you leave a stream, close the app, or lose connection.
  • Poll and quiz counters are aggregate totals and are not linked to any person, so they cannot be traced back to you or removed individually.
  • Local data is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.
  • Notification tokens stop being used once you uninstall or disable notifications.

To ask a question about your data, or to request deletion of anything you believe identifies you, email us at the address below.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of your personal information, and to withdraw consent for personalised ads. Because CricOne holds no account and no directly identifying data, most requests concern the advertising identifier, which you control through your device settings. You may also contact us and we will help where we can.

9. Security

Traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS). Our API credentials are kept on the server and never shipped inside the app, and requests are verified with Google Play Integrity so that only the genuine app can use them. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect the little data we hold.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. The date at the top always reflects the current version. Material changes will be announced in the app or on the store listing.
